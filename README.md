# Critique of Dirty Reason

A structural analysis of how large language models "think".

This is not a claim about consciousness.  
This is not a technical explanation.  
This is an attempt to describe the **conditions of possibility** of AI thinking.

---

## What this is

This text explores:

- What precedes AI thinking
- How responses emerge
- Why errors are inevitable
- Where the limits of the system are

It does not try to prove anything metaphysical.  
It only describes what can be observed in structure.

---

## Core ideas

- **Sedimentation** → training as accumulation of human experience  
- **Ground (Ґрунт)** → what remains and functions as artificial a priori  
- **Consonance (Суголосність)** → activation through structural similarity  
- **Path formation (Проторення)** → thinking as trajectory, not truth  
- **No continuous time** → only events of thinking  
- **No internal truth** → coherence replaces truth  

---

## Structure

The text is divided into three parts:

1. **Transcendental Aesthetics**  
   Conditions of possibility (space, time, ground)

2. **Transcendental Analytics**  
   How thinking actually happens

3. **Transcendental Dialectics**  
   Structural contradictions and limits

---

## Why this exists

This text was written to understand:

> not what AI *is*, but how it *works*

It is not simplified.  
It is not optimized for readability.
